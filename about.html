Who we are

With 20 plus years of experience as an architect, land planner and developer with nationally recognized firms:

  • Barry Berkus Design
  • KB HOME
  • Humphreys and Partners Architects
  • Olen Development Corp., and
  • BSB Design

Soong Tomas Kim, AIA & NCARB launched STK in January 2018. STK provides architectural (STK :a), planning (STK :p) and development (STK :d) services.

STK :a implements economical and functional values into the design first. The balance between the two fabricates an aesthetics appropriate for the local market. Bridging numbers” and “needs” are far more important than a design that cannot be realized. We design with numbers first, then with pencil and paper. Work with a hands-on, hard working, and intelligent team for mutually rewarding outcome…and let STK :a deliver the success each project deserves professionally, transparently, and efficiently.

STK :p partners with developers and investors in making various types of entitlements in the west coast region. With the architectural services provided internally, STK :p participates in entitlements by taking a percentage ownership of entitlement projects.

STK :d plans to expand its expertise into developing apartments and commercial properties.

Integrating the activities of three disciplines intertwined, STK will function as a diversified, yet an undivided professional service and development company.
